Word Jumble

Make as many words as you can in the grid in the allotted time!

Instructions:

1. Click and drag over adjacent tiles to make a word, release the mouse to end the word
2. The longer the word, the more points you get

Scoring System

  • 1 point for 3- or 4-letter words
  • 2 points for a 5-letter word
  • 3 points for a 6-letter word
  • 5 points for a 7-letter word
  • 11 points for a word more than 7-letters
